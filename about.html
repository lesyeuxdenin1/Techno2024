About Us

AwraGlow is a pioneering cosmetics company and online marketplace dedicated to promoting high-quality beauty products from local Filipino brands. Our mission is to illuminate natural beauty, support Filipino craftsmanship, and uphold sustainable practices. With a focus on inclusivity, quality, and environmental responsibility, we aim to redefine beauty standards in the Philippines. Our strengths lie in our local focus, quality assurance, commitment to sustainability, and diverse product range. By exclusively featuring Filipino brands, we support local entrepreneurs and celebrate Filipino creativity. We ensure all products meet high standards of safety, efficacy, and sustainability, with a strong commitment to eco-friendly practices.

Our target market comprises beauty-conscious individuals who prioritize quality, sustainability, and cultural resonance. These consumers show a strong inclination towards supporting local businesses and products that reflect Filipino heritage. They highly value eco-friendly products and brands that practice ethical sourcing and packaging. They tend to research and choose products based on quality, ingredients, and brand reputation, and they seek products that cater to diverse skin tones and types. Active engagement with brands that offer personalized experiences, exclusive launches, and community involvement is a key aspect of their buying behavior, heavily influenced by social media trends, reviews, and beauty influencers.
